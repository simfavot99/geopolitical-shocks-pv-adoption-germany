# geopolitical-shocks-pv-adoption-germany

A research project to measure the impact of the **2026 Iran war** on solar-PV adoption in Germany.

## What this is about

Did the 2026 Iran war (a geopolitical energy shock) measurably change how German households adopted solar-PV systems — in particular, small **balcony PVs**?

The project uses the German **MaStR** (Marktstammdatenregister) solar-unit registry as the unit of observation, tracks daily new registrations across the 16 federal states, and asks whether adoption sped up or slowed down around the war window (war start: **28 Feb 2026**, ceasefire: **8 Apr 2026**) compared to:

- the rest of the sample, and
- the same calendar window in earlier years (seasonality control).
